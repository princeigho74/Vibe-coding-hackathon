AI Dream-to-App Generator

Vibe Coding 4-3-2 Hackathon Project

1. Harnessing AI and Low-Code for Impact

Turning simple text prompts into working apps that solve real problems, empowering students and developers across Africa.

2. Hackathon Focus Areas

AI Study Buddy - Flashcard Generator

Frontend: HTML5/CSS/JS | Backend: Flask + MySQL | AI: Hugging Face QA API

Generate instant flashcards from study notes and save for reuse.

Mood Journal - AI Emotion Tracker

Frontend: HTML/CSS/JS + Charts | Backend: Flask + MySQL | AI: Hugging Face Sentiment Analysis

Track your emotions over time and visualize mood trends.

Recipe Recommender - Food Matcher

Frontend: HTML/CSS/JS | Backend: Flask + MySQL | AI: OpenAI API

Get quick recipes from ingredients you already have.

3. Challenge Statements Overview

  • Make studying fun and interactive with AI-generated flashcards.
  • Encourage mental wellness with automated emotion journaling.
  • Promote affordable healthy eating with AI-powered recipe suggestions.

4. Monetization with IntaSend

IntaSend enables secure, scalable payments across Africa without complex integration hurdles. Perfect for student-friendly apps.
